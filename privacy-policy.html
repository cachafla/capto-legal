Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 20, 2026

Capto ("the App") is developed by Boli Apps. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

When you sign in, we collect the following information from your Google or Apple account:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture (if provided by your sign-in provider)

We also store content you create and save while using the App:

  • Text notes, web links, PDF documents, and photos you capture
  • AI-generated summaries, tags, and categories for your content
  • Collections and extraction schemas you define
  • Checklists generated from your notes
  • Chat conversations with AI about your content

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the App's features:

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account
  • To save and organize your notes, collections, and checklists
  • To generate AI-powered summaries, tags, categories, and search answers
  • To enable chat with AI about your content
  • To extract structured data from your notes into collections

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Sign-In / Sign in with Apple — for authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Apple's Privacy Policy respectively.
  • OpenAI — to generate summaries, tags, categories, search answers, checklists, and chat responses. Your content is sent to OpenAI for processing. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

Your account data and content are stored on servers hosted by Fly.io in the United States. Data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time (see "Delete Your Account" below).

Subscriptions

The App offers a free tier and a paid Pro subscription. Subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store and managed via RevenueCat. We do not have access to your payment details.

Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the App: go to Settings → Delete Account. Deletion is permanent and removes all your notes, collections, checklists, and chat history from our servers.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact us at info@boliapps.com.
