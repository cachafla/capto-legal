Capto

AI-powered knowledge capture

Save anything and let AI do the rest. Capto turns your scattered notes, links, documents, and photos into an organized, searchable knowledge base.

Features

  • Capture anything — Save text notes, web links, PDFs, and photos from one unified interface
  • AI-powered organization — Every piece of content gets an automatic summary, relevant tags, and a category
  • Smart search — Full-text search plus AI-powered answers across your entire library with source citations
  • Custom collections — Define extraction schemas to pull structured data from your notes into organized collections
  • Chat with your content — Ask AI questions about any note or your entire library and get grounded answers
  • Checklists — Generate actionable checklists from any note and pin them for quick access
